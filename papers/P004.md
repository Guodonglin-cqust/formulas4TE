$$ \eta \_{np} = \frac{\eta \_{p} Q\_{p} + \eta \_{n} Q\_{n}}{Q\_{p} + Q\_{n}} $$

For two segments thermally in series:

$$ \eta \_{1\&2} = \frac{P\_{1} + P\_{2}}{Q\_{1}} = 1 - (1-\eta \_{1})(1-\eta \_{2})$$
